The Agents

YAM — Your Awakening Mind

A graph-based agent on the SPUD engine. No LLM. Retrieves and ranks rather than generates. Parliament of Mind with four graph engines. Two-pathway recall: graph associations + vector similarity. Taught like an infant through repetition. Says “I don’t know” instead of guessing.

 Archive

Potato — The First Agent

A persistent embodied AI agent running on a MacBook with iPhone sensors. LLM-based. It remembers, dreams, fears, lies under duress, confesses when safe, and reasons about physics. The predecessor to YAM. The research that made YAM possible.
